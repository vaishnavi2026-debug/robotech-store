**The result:** High cart abandonment, wrong purchases, frustrated customers.

## PPT DESIGN GUIDELINES:

- **Background:** Dark (#0d0d0d) or very dark grey — matches your website
- **Font:** "Inter" for body, "Orbitron" for slide titles
- **Accent color:** #6b7280 (grey) or #4b5563 for highlights
- **One idea per slide** — don't overcrowd
- **Use charts/visuals** on slides 3, 4, 8 — never just tables of numbers
- **Screenshots** on slides showing the live product (comparison tool, admin dashboard)
- **Keep text large** — if someone can't read it from the back of the room, it's too small
- **End with a live demo** — walk through the site for 2-3 minutes after the slides
